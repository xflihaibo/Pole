Added sophisticated animations to the homepage title "极点 (Pole)".
Key Features:
- **Slide-in Animation**: Chinese text "极点" slides down from above with a fade-in effect.
- **Gradient Animation**: The gradient background continuously shifts horizontally for a dynamic, flowing effect.
- **Shimmer Effect**: A light sweep animation passes over the text periodically, creating a premium, polished look.
- **Staggered Entrance**: The English text "(Pole)" appears slightly after the Chinese text with a scale-up effect.
- **Technical Implementation**: Used CSS keyframes, background-position animation, and pseudo-element `::after` for the shimmer effect.
